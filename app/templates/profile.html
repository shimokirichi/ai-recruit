How it works

Our codebase revolutionizes the hiring process by intelligently matching uploaded resumes to selected job roles using the power Artificial Intellegence. Steps:

  • Step #1 - Simply choose the desired job role from the predefined choices.
  • Step #2 - Open the application and use the "Upload and Analysis" button to select and upload multiple resume PDFs from your device.
  • Step #3 - The software will then extract data from the resumes and match them to the selected job role.
  • Step #4 - Eventually, you'll see a ranked list of candidates, each paired with relevant details such as match scores, contact information, and the resume
